Supported compilers

Asynchronous is tested and ok with:

  • gcc: >= 4.9

  • clang: >= 3.5

  • VS2015 with a limitation: BOOST_ASYNC_FUTURE/POST_MEMBER_1(or _2 or _3) as variadic macros are not supported

  • Intel ICC >= 13.
